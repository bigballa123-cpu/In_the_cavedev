About Us

"In the Cave" is an allegory by Plato, which is the extended metaphor behind our name. It is a simple story of two men bound to a cave in captivity since birth, but was finally freed by another. Once freed, they needed some adjusting to life outside the cave. Their eyes were burning, as all they saw for their whole lives turned out to be false! In a way, we are all held captive to our own caves. Exiting can feel impossible and eye damaging. Just like the allegory, we aspire to articulate cave exiting conversations that will leave a listener thinking. We talk about a lot, from psychology, financial literacy, life advice, and observations. Our guests include pioneers in their respective industries, breaking barriers and sharing wise words that if listened to, can form success stories. Have a listen and reach out to us for more information.
